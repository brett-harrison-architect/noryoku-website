JLPT Levels

From your first word to full fluency

Follow a clear path through all five JLPT proficiency levels. Unlock new challenges as you grow.

N5

Beginner

~80 kanji · ~600 vocab

Basic greetings, numbers, and everyday expressions.

N4

Elementary

~170 kanji · ~1,200 vocab

Simple conversations and basic reading comprehension.

N3

Intermediate

~370 kanji · ~3,500 vocab

Everyday situations and general topics in natural speed.

N2

Upper Intermediate

~1,000 kanji · ~6,000 vocab

Newspapers, lectures, and complex real-world content.

N1

Advanced

~2,000 kanji · ~10,000 vocab

Full professional fluency and academic-level comprehension.
